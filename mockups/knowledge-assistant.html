How does the DDA reversal process work?
bolt
Assistant
description 3 sources matched

The DDA (Direct Deposit Accounts) reversal process is a strictly audited procedure consisting of three primary steps:

1

Verification

Confirm the transaction status directly within the monitoring dashboard to ensure the original settlement was successful and is eligible for reversal.

2

Initiating

Submit the reversal request through the official operations portal. This step generates a cryptographic challenge that must be signed by the originating node.

3

Approval

A secondary authorization from a designated compliance officer is required for the final ledger settlement to prevent unauthorized fund movements.
